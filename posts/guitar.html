The Guitar

The guitar is a stringed musical instrument that is played by plucking or strumming the strings. It has been used for centuries in various music genres, from classical and flamenco to rock and pop.

History: The guitar has roots dating back over 4,000 years, with early versions appearing in Persia and Egypt. The modern guitar evolved from instruments like the lute and vihuela.

Types: There are different types of guitars, including acoustic, electric, and classical guitars.
